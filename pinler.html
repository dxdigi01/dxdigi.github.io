Use Case Scenarios

Market Campaigns

Do you know that a grocery store in your neighborhood offers discounts on vegetables every Friday? Save this information by pinning the store's location, adding a photo, and recording it as "Market" action type with "Friday Discount" name. Write "10% discount in vegetable section every Friday" in the content and share with the community.

🛒

Cafe Discoveries

Pin that amazing new cafe you discovered. Add atmosphere photos, take notes about special coffee varieties and share with friends. Other users can also add their experiences.

☕

Nature Walks

While hiking in nature, pin places where you see beautiful landscapes, interesting plants or animals. Document with photos and videos, revisit the same places in the future.

🏞️

Event Sharing

Pin events you attend like concerts, festivals, exhibitions. Share your experiences, create reminders for future events.
