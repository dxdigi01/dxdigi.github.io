Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Zero Data Collection & Absolute Privacy

Your privacy is our absolute highest priority. We strictly adhere to a "Zero Data Collection" policy. ShutdownGuard operates entirely locally on your machine. We do NOT collect, track, or access your personal files, browsing history, application usage, or keystrokes.

The only piece of information generated is an anonymous, cryptographic Device ID used solely to verify your 14-day trial and license status over our secure cloud network. We will never sell, share, or monetize any user data.

2. Payment Information

All payments are securely processed by our merchant of record, Paddle. We do not store your credit card information on our servers.

3. No Artificial Intelligence (AI)

We do not use Artificial Intelligence (AI) or machine learning models to process your data, monitor your behavior, or make background decisions. ShutdownGuard is a deterministic, 100% human-controlled software where you have absolute authority over every setting and schedule.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@dxdigi.com.
